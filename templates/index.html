Titanic Disaster Dataset

Description

The Titanic Disaster Dataset offers a detailed compilation of statistics pertaining to the passengers aboard the ill-fated RMS Titanic, providing a nuanced understanding of the tragic event. The dataset encompasses a range of information, including demographic details, socio-economic factors, and voyage-related particulars. This dataset serves as a valuable resource for exploring the dynamics of survival rates, investigating the impact of passenger characteristics on outcomes, and gaining insights into the socio-economic fabric of the Titanic's passengers.
